## Positioning

1DentalAI is not another phone tool, scribe, insurance checker, or dashboard. It is a dental AI operating system that connects calls, scheduling, insurance, RCM, clinical AI, reputation, analytics, and DSO operations into governed workflows.

Use this sentence everywhere:

> 1DentalAI helps dental teams turn calls, insurance, RCM, clinical notes, reviews, and analytics into one accountable AI-assisted workflow.

- scrape private groups

- use patient health condition targeting

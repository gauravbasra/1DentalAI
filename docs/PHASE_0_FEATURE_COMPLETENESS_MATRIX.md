## Engagement No-Send Contract

- `PatientEngagementEvent.status = APPROVED_TO_SEND` means staff approval for a future transport queue, not delivery.
- Staging engagement work always creates internal PMS work through `PmsTask` so the owning role can resolve consent, forms, recall, waitlist, no-show/cancel, post-op, reputation, or RCM handoffs.
- Consent and quiet hours are surfaced from `PmsPatientCommunicationPreference`; unresolved consent remains visible instead of being silently bypassed.
- Review requests can be blocked by `ReputationRecoveryCase` before any public reputation workflow proceeds.

## Launch-Readiness QA Harness

- `scripts/validate-phase0-depth.mjs` is the live matrix harness. It fails when a required product route, API endpoint, Prisma model, migration-backed table, matrix row, live-status cell, or no-fake-success rule is missing.
- Product route coverage currently includes `/app/phone`, `/app/engagement`, `/app/rcm`, `/app/reputation`, `/app/marketing`, `/app/connectors`, PMS core workbenches, appointment checkout, huddle, and Patient Finder.
- API coverage currently includes PMS patients/schedule/chart/perio/documents/insurance/ledger/tasks/treatment plans/appointment checkout, webchat widget/session/messages/settings/transcript/crawl, leads, workbenches, database health, and product health.
- Required DB table coverage is checked against both `prisma/schema.prisma` models and migration SQL so schema-only claims do not pass launch readiness.
- Live status is intentionally conservative: internal workflow surfaces can be live while external sends, calls, payer submissions, review posting, listing sync, PMS writeback, and campaign delivery remain staged or connector-blocked.

## Engagement No-Send Contract

- `PatientEngagementEvent.status = APPROVED_TO_SEND` means staff approval for a future transport queue, not delivery.
- Staging engagement work always creates internal PMS work through `PmsTask` so the owning role can resolve consent, forms, recall, waitlist, no-show/cancel, post-op, reputation, or RCM handoffs.
- Consent and quiet hours are surfaced from `PmsPatientCommunicationPreference`; unresolved consent remains visible instead of being silently bypassed.
- Approval can apply `BLOCKED_CONSENT`, `BLOCKED_QUIET_HOURS`, `BLOCKED_FORMS_INTAKE`, `BLOCKED_MEDICAL_REVIEW`, or `BLOCKED_PRE_MED_REVIEW`; blocked states are internal work states, not failed external sends.
- Booking request, waitlist, recall, form/intake, and post-op actions only stage internal follow-up from PMS records; appointment writeback, form-link delivery, and SMS/email transport require later connector proof.
- Review requests can be blocked by `ReputationRecoveryCase` before any public reputation workflow proceeds.

## Phone No-Send Contract

- `PhoneOutboundMessage.approvalStatus = APPROVED_STAGED` means staff approval for a future connector queue, not delivery.
- `PhoneOutboundMessage.deliveryStatus = BLOCKED_CONNECTOR_REQUIRED` means consent may be approved, but the SMS, payment, forms, or scheduling connector is not ready.
- Payment links and forms links are stored as `linkType`, `linkTargetId`, `linkLabel`, and `readiness` metadata only; this repo does not fabricate external URLs.
- PMS screen pop context is read from patient, appointment, ledger, treatment, recall, forms, task, and communication preference records before staff stages a call/SMS handoff.
- AI call summaries can be sent to `CHART_NOTE_REVIEW`, which creates internal phone and PMS tasks; it does not claim clinical chart writeback until staff review occurs.
- Twilio inbound voice, status, recording, transcription, and SMS webhooks create PMS-linked conversation/tasks/audit records only; they do not claim outbound calling, texting, transfer, hold, or AI voice completion until the carrier connector is validated.

## RCM No-Submission Contract

- `APPROVED_STAGED`, `RECOVERY_STAGED`, and payer-contact updates mean an internal packet or work queue is ready for staff/connector handling, not that a payer received it.
- Claim, prior authorization, denial appeal, payer follow-up, and revenue recovery flows stay in `CONNECTOR_REQUIRED`, `BLOCKED_CONNECTOR_REQUIRED`, or `MANUAL_PROOF_REQUIRED` until clearinghouse, payer portal, 276/277, ERA/EOB, or manual staff proof is attached.
- ERA/EOB posting is the only RCM workflow that writes PMS ledger/payment rows here, and it requires a positive payment plus an ERA trace or EOB document reference before posting.
